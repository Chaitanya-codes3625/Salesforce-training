# Salesforce CRM Basics

A beginner-friendly introduction to Salesforce CRM and how it helps businesses manage customer relationships.

---

## 🚀 What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform that helps businesses:

- Manage customers and sales
- Track leads and opportunities
- Store data securely
- Automate business processes
- Generate reports and dashboards

Salesforce can be accessed from anywhere using desktop or mobile devices.

---

## 📌 What is CRM?

CRM stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.

It helps businesses:
- Manage customer and prospect information
- Track interactions and communication
- Improve team collaboration
- Organize sales and customer data

---

## 🗂 Salesforce Data Structure

| Term | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Object** | A table that stores data |
| **Record** | A single row of data |
| **Field** | A column that stores a value |
| **Org** | Your Salesforce environment |
| **App** | A collection of tools and features |

### Example
- Spreadsheet Tab → Object  
- Spreadsheet Row → Record  
- Spreadsheet Column → Field  

---

## 📦 Standard Salesforce Objects

### Accounts
Companies or businesses you work with.

### Contacts
People associated with an Account.

### Leads
Potential customers who are not yet qualified.

### Opportunities
Qualified deals or sales opportunities.

---

## ⚙️ Custom Objects

Salesforce allows you to create **Custom Objects** based on business needs.

### Examples
- Projects
- Employee Requests
- Inventory Tracking

---

- Cloud-based and secure
- Easy to customize

Salesforce CRM helps businesses manage customer relationships efficiently by organizing data, automating processes, and improving collaboration.
